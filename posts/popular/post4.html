Building Better Evaluation Dashboards for LLMs

Observability is the difference between shipping confidently and flying blind. Here's how to design dashboards that your whole team trusts.

Start with a Narrative

Dashboards should tell a story: what changed, why it matters, and what to do next. Group charts into sections for quality, safety, and performance.

Key Widgets

  • Metric trends: Track accuracy, hallucination rate, and user satisfaction over time.
  • Slice explorer: Segment results by persona, language, or channel.
  • Outlier inbox: Surface the 10 most surprising failures for manual review.

Implementation Tips

Use a warehouse-first approach with dbt modeling metrics, then visualize in Metabase or Lightdash. Automate ingestion from your eval harness nightly.
