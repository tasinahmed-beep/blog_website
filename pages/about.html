Behind the Publication

AI Pulse is a daily logbook of experiments, systems, and stories from the front lines of artificial intelligence.

The Mission

AI changes faster than any other field I have worked in. Shipping responsibly means pairing curiosity with tight feedback loops. AI Pulse exists to translate breakthroughs into practical workflows you can use today.

Every post blends three pillars: clear explanation, reproducible steps, and lessons from real deployments. Whether you are prototyping agents, modernizing existing products, or leading an AI team, my goal is to give you the tactical detail you need to make decisions quickly.

What You'll Find

  • Rapid tutorials for orchestrating LLMs, vector search, and automation pipelines.
  • Breakdowns of research drops and benchmarks—framed so you can apply the findings.
  • Playbooks on product strategy, measurement, and team culture for AI organizations.
  • Reviews of the tools and infra that actually hold up when customers are depending on you.

How I Work

I split my time between building AI-powered products, advising startups, and teaching workshops. The site is updated daily with fresh notes: sometimes it's a deep-dive, sometimes it's a quick win discovered while debugging a model. Either way, it is grounded in firsthand experience.

If you have a topic you want explored or a product you'd like help evaluating, reach out. The best ideas always start with a conversation.

Quick Facts

  • Role: Product engineer & AI strategist
  • Base: Remote-first, collaborating worldwide
  • Focus Areas: LLM apps, evaluation, human-in-the-loop systems
  • Community: Mentor & speaker at AI meetups and hackathons
